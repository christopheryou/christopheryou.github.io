Projects

  • Clinical Trial Enrollment Project

    R24: Clinical Trial Enrollment Project

    May 2021

    Virtual Humans

    This NIH-funded project seeks to use Virtual Human Technology to increase clinical trial participation among older adult minorities (Black/African American, Hispanic/Latin, and/or rural adults over the age of 50). We designed the virtual human ALEX to be culturally sensitive, track engagement in NIH-funded trials, and compare ALEX and standard recruitment methods. Ultimately, our goal is to create an adaptable virtual human intervention to increase minority participation rates in NIH-funded clinical trials. We use virtual characters to educate users and provide them with decisionâ€“making abilities regarding their health. We will also assist users in identifying suitable clinical trials to discuss with their doctors.

    Technologies: HTML, JavaScript, CSS, Unreal Engine, MetaHumans, Machine Learning Models, Python, ChatGPT
